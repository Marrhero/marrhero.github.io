Game Dev Portfolio

Do you have something you'd like me to work on? Send me a message at marrsadiq@gmail.com and let's talk!

Chroma

Chroma is a high intensity color matching puzzle game released in 2019 on Android. The game was developed with C# in the Unity Game Engine. I worked on a two man team for the development of this game. I acted as the lead artist and I was responsible for all aspects of the visual design. I created all the art assets and led the art direction of the game, working to create visual experiences that matched up to the tracks created by our musician. The neon colors and flashy visual effects were specifically designed to give a high octane feel to the gameplay. I made use of the Unity Profiler to optimize the game's performance, and ensure that it ran smoothly on a variety of devices, especially low-end devices, without compromising the high fidelity audiovisual experience that we were aiming for.

Watch gameplay here.

Engine: Unity

Language: C#

Platform: Android

Roles: Artist, Game Designer, Programmer

Blockhead

Blockhead was developed with the Cocos2d-x engine. Our goal was to create a challenging game, and we ended up with quite a few players competing for the top position on the Google Play leaderboard. I was the lead artist and art director for the game, and I created all of the art with vector tools to give the game a friendly cartoony appearance that hid just how tough this game was to beat.

Engine: Cocos2d-x

Language: C++

Platform: Android

Roles: Artist, Programmer

Flick!

This was the first game I ever developed and shipped, as part of our two-person studio Indecyfer. The concept was simple enough; flick the spheres into the corresponding corner before they vanished. Despite it's simplicity, I learned a lot about the full cylce of game production.

Engine: Cocos2d-x

Language: C++

Platform: Android

Roles: Artist, Game Designer, Programmer
